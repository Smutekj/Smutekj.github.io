Constrained Delaunay Triangulation

A constrained version of the Delaunay triangulation based on the article of Sloan. Includes option to choose type of position vector including small types, like unsigned char. This can be useful for optimisation in RTS games for instance, where maps tend to lie on not too large grids. (For example, Starcraft 2 uses 2 bytes per Vertex coordinate). Can be used for:

  • PathFinding
  • Vision polygon construction
  • Terrain construction (I Guess you could use it for that :))
  • Discretisation of the physical domain in FEM/FVM simulations
  • Voronoi diagrams construction?
